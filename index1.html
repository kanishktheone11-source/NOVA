What We Do

Engineering Tomorrow's Spacecraft

From propulsion systems to satellite design, we're hands-on building the technologies that will take humanity to the stars.

Rocket Propulsion

Design and test liquid and solid propellant engines. Our propulsion team has successfully fired over 20 engine tests this year.

Learn more

Satellite Systems

Build CubeSats and small satellites for Earth observation and communication. Currently developing our 3rd orbital mission.

Learn more

Mission Control

Develop ground station software and tracking systems. Our control room monitors active satellites 24/7.

Our Projects

Missions In Progress

Explore our active projects spanning propulsion research, orbital mechanics, and beyond.

Propulsion

Project Helios

A bi-propellant liquid engine capable of 5kN thrust. Targeting our first integrated flight test in Spring 2026.

Satellite

NovaSat-3

Our third CubeSat mission featuring advanced Earth imaging sensors and an experimental ion thruster.

Research

Lunar Habitat Study

Collaborative research on sustainable life support systems for long-duration lunar missions.
