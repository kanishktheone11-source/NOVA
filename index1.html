Our Mission

Turning Moon Dust into Habitats

We are shifting from a 'there and back again' mindset to 'there to stay' by building lunar infrastructure using the lunar regolith.

Laser Sintering

Our Laser VMX process uses high-energy lasers to transform raw lunar regolith into high-density, load-bearing ceramic structures without water or binders.

Autonomous Robotics

Utilizing mobile platforms with six degrees of freedom (DOF) to autonomously excavate, sift, and 3D print toolpaths without human intervention.

Class III Architecture

Building hybrid systems that combine Earth-made inflatable cores with continuously 3D-printed ISRU protective shells for radiation and MMOD safety.

Current Missions

Active Infrastructure Projects

From landing pads to pressurized habitats, we are engineering the foundation of the lunar economy.

Horizontal

Project Landing Pad

Developing "Sunflower Vault" patterns to redirect supersonic rocket ejecta and protect nearby infrastructure from damage.

Vertical

Lunar Lantern

A vertical pressurized habitat utilizing parabolic base isolators and post-tensioning cables to survive lunar seismic activity.

Protection

Whipple Shielding

Modular secondary systems designed for micrometeoroid and orbital debris (MMOD) protection of vertical structures.
